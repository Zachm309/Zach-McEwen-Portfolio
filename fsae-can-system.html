Fake Battery Segments for BMS Operation

The battery management system expects voltage drops across battery segments, otherwise it will crowd the CAN bus with error messages. At the time of testing the battery was not available, so we simulated this by powering eight resistors with 30 V and wiring a voltage tap after each resistor.
